# Complex Numbers

## Basic
i² = -1
z = a + bi
|z| = √(a² + b²)

## Operations
(a+bi) + (c+di) = (a+c) + (b+d)i
(a+bi)(c+di) = (ac-bd) + (ad+bc)i

## Polar Form
z = r(cos θ + i sin θ) = re^(iθ)
De Moivre: (re^(iθ))^n = r^n e^(inθ)

## Quadratic with D < 0
Roots are complex conjugates: (-b ± i√|D|)/(2a)
