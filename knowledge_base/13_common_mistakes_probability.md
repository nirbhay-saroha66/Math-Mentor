# Common Mistakes - Probability

## Conditional Probability
- P(A|B) ≠ P(B|A) in general
- P(A and B) ≠ P(A) × P(B) unless independent

## Counting
- Permutation vs Combination: order matters in P(n,r)
- With replacement vs without affects total outcomes

## Common Errors
- Treating "or" as exclusive when it might be inclusive
- Forgetting to subtract overlap in P(A ∪ B)
