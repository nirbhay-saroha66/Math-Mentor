# Common Mistakes - Calculus

## Limits
- Applying L'Hôpital when not 0/0 or ∞/∞
- Forgetting limit exists only if LHL = RHL
- Confusing lim (f+g) with lim f + lim g when limits don't exist

## Derivatives
- Chain rule: forgetting to multiply by inner derivative
- Product rule: wrong order in quotient rule formula
- d/dx(x^x) requires logarithmic differentiation

## Optimization
- Not checking endpoints
- Confusing local vs global extrema
